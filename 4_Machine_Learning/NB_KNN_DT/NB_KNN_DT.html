1. Maximum Posteriori Hypothesis (10 points)

When a test for steroids is given to soccer players, 98% of the players taking steroids test positive and 12% of the players not taking steroids test positive. Suppose that 5% of soccer players take steroids. What is the maximum posteriori hypothesis for a soccer player who tests positive? What are the exact posterior probabilities?

Max Posteriori Formula ($h_{MAP}$)

$h_{MAP} = arg\,max_{h_i \in H} P(D|h)P(h)$

$h = steroids | \neg steroids$

$D = + | -$

Given: $P(steroids) = 0.05, \, P(\neg steroids) = 0.95$

Q: What are the exact posterior probabilities?

$P(+|\neg steroids) = 0.12 \; (false\;positive), \; P(-|\neg steroids) = 0.88 \; (true\;positive)$

$P(+|steroids) = 0.98 \; (true\,positive), \, P(-|steroids) = 0.02 \; (false\,negative)$

Q: What is the maximum posteriori hypothesis for a soccer player who tests positive?

$P(+|steroids)*P(steroids) = (0.98)(0.05) = 0.049 \approx 5\%$

$P(+|\neg steroids)*P(\neg steroids) = (0.12)(0.95) = 0.114 \approx 11\%$

$h_{MAP} = arg\,max_{h_i \in H} \{5\%, 11\%\}$

$\implies 11\% > 5\%$

$\therefore \, h_{MAP} = \neg steroids$

Naive Bayes Classifier [$P(c|x)$]

$P(c|x) = \frac{P(x|c)P(c)}{P(x)}\, = \, P(x_1|c) \, \cdot P(x_2|c) \, \cdot \, ... \, \cdot \, P(x_n|c) \, \cdot \, P(c)$

Given:

$c = Play|\neg Play$

$x_a =\, < Sunny, Mild, Normal, Weak >$

$x_b =\, < Rain, Cool, High, Strong >$

a) $x_a =\, < Sunny, Mild, Normal, Weak >$

$P(c|x_a) = (Play \,| <Sunny, Mild, Normal, Weak>)$

$P(Play|x_{a,i}) = P(Sunny|Play) \,\cdot\, P(Mild|Play) \,\cdot\, P(Normal|Play) \,\cdot\, P(Weak|Play) \,\cdot\, P(Play)$

$= \frac{1}{4} \,\cdot\, \frac{1}{4} \,\cdot\, \frac{7}{8} \,\cdot\, \frac{3}{8} \,\cdot\, \frac{4}{7}$

$= 0.25 \,\cdot\, 0.25 \,\cdot\, 0.875 \,\cdot\, 0.375 \,\cdot\, 0.571 \, = 0.01171 \approx 1.17\%$

$=\frac{0.01171}{0.01171 + 0.0475} = \frac{0.01171}{0.0592} = 0.1978 \approx 19.8\%$

$(\neg Play \,| <Sunny, Mild, Normal, Weak>)$

$P(\neg Play|x_{a,i}) = P(Sunny|\neg Play) \,\cdot\, P(Mild|\neg Play) \,\cdot\, P(Normal|\neg Play) \,\cdot\, P(Weak|\neg Play) \,\cdot\, P(\neg Play)$

$= \frac{2}{3} \,\cdot\, \frac{1}{2} \,\cdot\, \frac{1}{2} \,\cdot\, \frac{2}{3} \,\cdot\, \frac{3}{7}$

$= 0.667 \,\cdot\, 0.5 \,\cdot\, 0.5 \,\cdot\, 0.667 \,\cdot\, 0.428 \, = 0.0475 \approx 4.76\%$

$=\frac{0.0475}{0.01171 + 0.0475} = \frac{0.0475}{0.0592} = 0.8023 \approx 80.2\%$

$80.2\% > 19.8\%$

$\therefore x_a = \neg Play$

b) $x_b =\, <Rain, Cool, High, Strong >$

$P(c|x_b) = (Play \,| <Rain, Cool, High, Strong>)$

$P(Play|x_{b,i}) = P(Rain|Play) \,\cdot\, P(Cool|Play) \,\cdot\, P(High|Play) \,\cdot\, P(Strong|Play) \,\cdot\, P(Play)$

$= \frac{3}{8} \,\cdot\, \frac{3}{8} \,\cdot\, \frac{1}{8} \,\cdot\, \frac{5}{8} \,\cdot\, \frac{4}{7}$

$= 0.375 \,\cdot\, 0.375 \,\cdot\, 0.125 \,\cdot\, 0.625 \,\cdot\, 0.571 \, = 0.00627 \approx 0.63\%$

$=\frac{0.00627}{0.00627 + 0.00396} = \frac{0.00627}{0.01023} = 0.6129 \approx 61.3\%$

$(\neg Play \,| <Rain, Cool, High, Strong>)$

$P(\neg Play|x_{a,i}) = P(Rain|\neg Play) \,\cdot\, P(Cool|\neg Play) \,\cdot\, P(High|\neg Play) \,\cdot\, P(Strong|\neg Play) \,\cdot\, P(\neg Play)$

$= \frac{1}{3} \,\cdot\, \frac{1}{6} \,\cdot\, \frac{1}{2} \,\cdot\, \frac{1}{3} \,\cdot\, \frac{3}{7}$

$= 0.333 \,\cdot\, 0.1667 \,\cdot\, 0.5 \,\cdot\, 0.333 \,\cdot\, 0.428 \, = 0.00396 \approx 0.40\%$

$=\frac{0.00396}{0.00627 + 0.00396} = \frac{0.00396}{0.01023} = 0.3871 \approx 38.7\%$

$61.2\% > 38.7\%$

$\therefore x_b = Play$

$E(S) = \sum_{i=1}^c - \,p_i log_2 p_i$

Entropy (Multi Attributes) [$E(T,X)$]

$E(T,X) = \sum_{c \in X} P(c) E(c) = P(X_1) \cdot c_1 log_2 c_1 + P(X_2) \cdot c_2 log_2 c_2 + ... + P(X_n) \cdot c_n log_2 c_n$

Information Gain [$G(T,X)$]

$G(T,X) = E(T) - E(T,X)$

Best Split Point [$SplitInfo_A(D)$]

$SplitInfo_A(D) = \sum_{j=1}^v \frac{|D_j|}{|D|} \cdot log_2 (\frac{|D_j|}{|D|})$

Gain Ratio [$GainRatio(A)$]

$GainRatio(A) = \frac{Gain(A)}{SplitInfo(A)}$

Play, Outlook

$E(Play, Outlook) = P(Outlook) \cdot E(Outlook_{i_{p,\neg p}})$

$ = P(Sunny) \cdot E(Sunny_p, Sunny_{\neg p}) + P(Overcast) \cdot E(Overcast_p, Overcast_{\neg p}) + P(Rain) \cdot E(Rain_p, Rain_{\neg p})$

$ = (0.4)(1) + (0.1)(0.918) + (.5)(0.971) = 0.9773$

$G(Play,Outlook) = E(Play) - E(Play,Outlook) = E(5,5) - 0.9773 = 1 - 0.9773 = 0.0227 \approx 0.02$

$E(Play, Temperature) = P(Temperature) \cdot E(Temperature_{i_{p,\neg p}})$

$ = P(Hot) \cdot E(Hot_p, Hot_{\neg p}) + P(Mild) \cdot E(Mild_p, Mild_{\neg p}) + P(Cool) \cdot E(Cool_p, Cool_{\neg p})$
